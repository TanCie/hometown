~~~ Top three sights to visit in Durgapur ~~~

Junction Mall

Junction Mall is located right at the intersection of Durgapur’s commercial centre and NH2. The mall has become a retail hallmark in South Bengal and Rank’s no.1 on Tripadvisor’s Places to Visit in Durgapur, West Bengal.

Fortune Deli

A casual 24-hour eatery, a lobby cafe and a bar. A rooftop terrace features a gym, a pool, and a spa area with a sauna and a steam room. Parking is available.

Durgapur Barrage

It is built across the Damodar River at Durgapur in Paschim Bardhaman district and partly in Paschim Bardhaman district, in the Indian state of West Bengal. It was constructed by Damodar Valley Corporation mainly for the purpose of irrigation and also to supply water to Industrial township of Durgapur.
